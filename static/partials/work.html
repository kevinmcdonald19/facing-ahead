6. Work/Home Issues
Why do we ask about Works & Family Issues?

Work & Family Issues

Most of us are swamped with many things and we need to take responsibility for setting a healthy balance and making sure we keep the marriage as our top priority. Eventually there may be children, jobs, or other things that can come between the couple. It is up to the couple to take responsibility for how they will handle the various life obligations that will be an ongoing part of their relationship.

Many engaged and newly married couples report that even before marriage they have trouble balancing "together time" with the many other time-consuming activities in their lives.
